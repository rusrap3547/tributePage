Tetsuya Nomura

1970 - Present

Tetsuya Nomura is a Japanese video game artist, designer, producer, and director working for Square Enix. He was hired by Square initially as a monster designer for Final Fantasy V, before being shifted towards secondary character designer alongside Yoshitaka Amano for Final Fantasy VI.


"The closer we are to the light, the greater our shadow becomes, The heart may be weak. And sometimes it may even give in. But I've learned that deep down, there's a light that never goes out!" - Tetsuya Nomura
